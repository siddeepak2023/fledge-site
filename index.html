FAQ

Questions, answered.

Which platforms does Fledge work with? +

YouTube, TikTok, Twitch, and Instagram. Drop an export — Fledge auto-detects your columns and computes your metrics in the browser.

Is it really built for streamers? +

Yes — it works for any creator, but it’s sharpest for streamers: chat-hype clip detection finds your highest-energy moments from chat spikes and flags exactly what to clip and repurpose.

Is my channel data safe? +

Your export is parsed entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server, nothing is stored, and nothing is used to train a model.

Do I need to pay to try it? +

No. The free tier gives you core metrics, the content screener, and 5 AI questions a month — no card required.
